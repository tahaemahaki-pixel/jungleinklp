Meet Albatross

Coverup tattoos are an important part of what I do. Removing the damage and trauma caused by botched tattoos and more importantly restoring self esteem and confidence in my client. Replacing trauma of the past with a positive uplifting experience locked into place through healing ink.

Other times it's just really cool to bring a clients vision to life for them. I don't really focus on myself as the hero in the story, for me, it's all about my client. They're the Hero it's their life, it's their journey, their story they will carry into eternity. I'm just the facilitator, the Ferry man you pay to get you from this place to the next in your life changing transformation through Tattoo.

The art of Tatau for me is a service to community and a role I inherited from my ancestors. It's a role I grew into and like my markings, earnt over decades. Although its roots are ancient I share my gift through its modern personification.
